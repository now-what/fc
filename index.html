About

I am an Italian journalist and SEO professional, living as a digital nomad since 2012: I house sit and work from beautiful locations around the world. I manage several online communities, and I write articles and features (in Italian). I am available to write columns and talk about alternative and sustainable lifestyles and travels.

I also help small local businesses, artists and bands to establish and manage the presence and the appearance of their brand on the internet: from a simple, clean website setup, to social media management; from local SEO strategies to advertising campaigns. I also provide copy writing, PR and corporate communication services.

Deep Purple Italia

This is the official Italian Deep Purple fan club, started in 2001. The community includes news, discographies, concert and event information, and the biggest non-English Deep Purple and related forum. It is the point of reference for Italian fans for all things Deep Purple, and it is often quoted in the music press inside and outside of Italy.

  • Project: Deep Purple Italia. In collaboration with Stefano Serafinelli.
  • Date: Since 2005
  • Service: Planning and strategy. News and content. SEO. Partnerships and affiliations. Media relations.
